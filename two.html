Tourist Atrractions

Kolkata is blessed with the amalgamation of Colonial and Modern architecture with a vivid culture which offers a great experience to visitors. The city of Kolkata along with its suburbs which is known as Greater Kolkata offers Historical Buildings, Monuments, Museums, Unique Bridges over the Hooghly River, Serene Lakes and Refreshing Gardens, Ancient Temples and Churches, some of the largest Sports Stadium.

Here are glimpses of Important Places to Visit:

                                                        Victoria Memorial                                             Howrah Bridge                                                       Vidyasagar Setu

                                                An iconic landmark of Kolkata             6th Largest Cantilever Bridge of the world         One of the longest bridges in Asia

                                                    Dakhineswar Temple                                                   Belur Math                                                 St. Paul's Cathedral

                                    Iconic landmark in the bank of the river           Ramakrishna Mission Headquaters                           A Historical Church

                                                        Writer's Building                                                       Raj Bhavan                                                 Kolkata University

                                                  Government Secretariat                                           Governor Residence

There are many more places to visit as Sahid Minar (Martyr’s Monument), Kolkata Museum, Eden Garden – iconic Cricket Stadium, Salt Lake Stadium (one of the largest soccer stadiums in the world), the Botanical Garden spread over 270 acres of land, Dhakuria Lake – a beautiful Serene Lake inside the city and more.
